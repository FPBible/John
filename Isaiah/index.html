Isaiah

New Creation Selections

Important note:
This is not a complete paraphrase of the book of Isaiah. Only selected chapters that directly announce, frame, or anticipate the New Creation fulfilled in Christ are included here.

Isaiah functions as the primary prophetic bridge between the Old Covenant and the New Creation reality revealed in Christ. The selected chapters below are treated as covenantal prophecy, fulfilled and realised under the New Covenant, not as futurist prediction.

Included Chapters

  • Isaiah 11 — The New Humanity Under the Messiah (Coming Soon)
