Reading Notes

How FPBible keeps Matthew from drifting into futurism.
  • Audience relevance first: Matthew is written into Israel’s first-century covenant crisis.
  • Time statements stay time statements: “near,” “soon,” and “this generation” are anchors, not elastic words.
  • Hebrew idiom: cosmic collapse language is covenant-judgment imagery, not planet-destruction.
  • Olivet anchors everything: Matthew 24–25 locks the framework for the whole book.
