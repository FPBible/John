Ephesians

Fulfilled Paraphrase · One New Humanity · Life in Christ

Ephesians announces what life looks like after reconciliation is complete. It is not a warning letter. It is a declaration of identity, unity, and vocation for people who already belong in Christ.

This letter moves from:

  • who you are in Christ
  • what has been accomplished
  • how the new humanity now walks together
