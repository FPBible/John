Hebrews

Fulfilled Paraphrase + Commentary (New Creation Lens)

Hebrews addresses Jewish believers living in the final days of the old covenant world. The Son has spoken, the sacrifice is finished, and the temple system is about to be shaken. The message is urgent: do not retreat into what is passing away.

This letter explains why Christ is better than angels, Moses, priests, sacrifices, and the entire covenant structure that preceded Him.
