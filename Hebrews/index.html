Hebrews

Fulfilled Paraphrase + Commentary (New Creation Lens)

Hebrews is written to Jewish believers under pressure to retreat back into the old covenant system — temple, priesthood, sacrifices, and the safety of the familiar. The message is blunt: the Son is God’s final word, His once-for-all sacrifice is complete, and the old covenant world is about to be shaken.

This book is not about the end of the physical universe. It is about the end of a covenant administration and the unveiling of the New Covenant world in Christ.
