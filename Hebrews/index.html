Hebrews

Fulfilled Paraphrase · Covenant Transition · New Creation

Hebrews is written at the close of the Old Covenant world. Its purpose is not speculation about distant futures, but decisive exhortation during a collapsing system.

The letter presses one question relentlessly: Will you remain with Christ, or retreat into what is passing away?

How This Book Is Read

  • Warnings are covenantal, not psychological
  • Judgment language is historical, not post-mortem
  • “Falling away” means returning to the Law
  • “The Day” refers to imminent covenant closure
  • The New Covenant is complete and unshakable
