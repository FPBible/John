Revelation

Fulfilled Paraphrase · Covenant Judgment · New Creation Unveiled

Revelation is apocalyptic covenant prophecy written to first-century churches under pressure. It unveils Jesus as present King, judges the persecuting old covenant order, and announces the public arrival of New Covenant reality. This book is not an end-times chart factory. It is a time-framed, audience-locked message of endurance, discernment, and vindication.

Key tension: the churches are tempted to fear, blend, or bow. Revelation does not give escapism. It gives Christ-centered clarity: “soon / near,” prophetic idioms, covenant judgment on the old world, and the New Creation people standing in the Lamb’s victory.
