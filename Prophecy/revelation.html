Revelation

Apocalypse as Covenant Unveiling

Reading Frame
Revelation unveils Christ’s victory over the old covenant powers. Symbols are drawn from the Old Testament.
  • Babylon = apostate Jerusalem
  • Beasts = covenant authorities
  • Lake of fire = judgment imagery
  • New Jerusalem = New Covenant people

Revelation ends with **presence**, not postponement: “Behold, the dwelling of God is with humanity.”
