Luke 21

The Days of Vengeance

Reading Frame
Luke explicitly names this period “the days of vengeance” — covenant judgment on Jerusalem for rejecting the prophets and the Son.

Luke’s account removes ambiguity by stating plainly: “These are the days of vengeance, so that all things which are written may be fulfilled.” (Luke 21:22)

Key Clarification
This is not the end of the planet. It is the fulfillment of written covenant judgments, culminating in Jerusalem’s fall (AD 70).
  • Jerusalem surrounded by armies → historical marker
  • Wrath on “this people” → covenant scope
  • Times of the Gentiles fulfilled → transition completed
