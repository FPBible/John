Prophecy Hub

Fulfilled Judgment · Covenant Transition · New Creation

Biblical prophecy does not describe the end of the physical universe. It announces covenant judgment, the removal of the old covenant order, and the establishment of the new covenant world in Christ.

Hub rule: if a passage sounds future without clarification, it fails audit. Chapters link here; the Hub carries the weight.

How This Hub Works

This hub is organised into two complementary layers:

  • Framework pages — establish how prophetic language works across Scripture (covenant, temple-world, idiom)
  • Textual deep dives — disputed passages handled line-by-line without importing later assumptions

Framework gives orientation. Deep dives give control. Both are necessary.

How to Read Prophetic Language

Prophetic imagery is interpreted by Scripture itself. Where the New Testament explains the meaning of:

  • judgment
  • resurrection
  • kingdom
  • new creation

those explanations govern how prophetic language is read. Symbol is not dismissed — it is decoded by covenant context.
