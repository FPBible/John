Daniel

Kingdom, Son of Man, and Time

Reading Frame
Daniel is not a distant future timetable. It is a compressed vision of empire, judgment, and kingdom culminating in Messiah’s reign.

The “Son of Man coming with the clouds” (Dan 7) describes **enthronement**, not descent. Jesus applies this directly to Himself.

  • Beasts = empires
  • Clouds = divine authority
  • Kingdom = already given to the Son
Time Statements
Daniel’s “time, times, and half a time” and “seventy weeks” terminate in first-century fulfilment, not endless postponement.

Daniel undergirds Matthew 24, Philippians 2–3, and Revelation.
