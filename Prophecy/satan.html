4) Zechariah 3: Accusation Resisted, Restoration Enacted

Zechariah 3 presents a covenantal courtroom scene focused on standing, accusation, and restoration. The high priest is in view, clothed in defilement, while the accuser stands to prosecute and oppose renewal.
