Fulfilled Paraphrase Bible

New Creation Lens · Covenant Transition · Zero Futurism

FPBible is a modern paraphrase and commentary project built to keep Scripture within its original covenant horizon, while applying the finished reality of Christ clearly, directly, and without delay theology.

Project rule: if a passage sounds like it is “still pending” without explanation, it fails and is rewritten.

Living in the New Creation

Christian faith is not defined by waiting, countdowns, or speculation. Its center is presence — a settled life grounded in what Christ has already accomplished.

FPBible reads Scripture through completion rather than postponement. We let time-statements speak plainly, treat prophetic language as prophetic (Hebrew idiom, covenant imagery), and live from access already given, not access still to be earned.

  • Presence, not distance — God with us in ordinary life.
  • Fulfillment, not delay — promises matured into lived reality.
  • Identity, not anticipation — we are new, not nearly new.
  • Spirit, not performance — life expressed, not maintained by fear.
  • Clarity, not frenzy — prophecy handled once, then linked via the Hub.
