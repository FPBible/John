FP Bible

Fulfilled Paraphrase · Covenant Transition · New Creation

The FP Bible reads Scripture in its own historical and covenantal context. It takes audience relevance seriously, recognises fulfilled prophetic language, and presents New Covenant life as a present, lived reality in Christ — not a postponed hope or an end-times system.
