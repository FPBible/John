FP Bible

Fulfilled Paraphrase · New Creation Lens

A covenant-focused Bible project that reads Scripture within its historical horizon, recognising the completion of the Old Covenant order and the present reality of life in Christ.

Gospels

  • John
    A theological Gospel announcing life, light, judgment, and glory as present realities fulfilled in Christ and embodied in the New Creation.

Letters

  • Romans
    Righteousness apart from the law, union in Christ, and the formation of a new humanity beyond ethnic and covenant boundaries.
  • Hebrews
    The decisive end of the sacrificial system and the opening of direct access to God through the finished work of Christ.
  • Colossians
    Christ as the fullness of God, exposing the emptiness of shadow-based religion and affirming the believer’s completeness in Him.
  • Ephesians
    Life from a seated position in Christ, revealing the one new humanity formed through reconciliation and peace.
  • Philippians
    Joy, humility, and stability rooted in Christ’s present reign, written from confinement yet marked by confidence and peace.

Hebrew Scriptures

  • Isaiah
    Covenant judgment, restoration, and new-creation language that shapes how Jesus and the New Testament speak about kingdom, resurrection, and renewal.

Prophecy Hub

  • Prophecy Hub
    A guided framework for reading biblical prophecy as covenant judgment, transition of ages, and fulfilled New Creation realities — not predictions of the end of the physical universe.

Salvation

  • What Salvation Means
    Salvation explained as deliverance, union with Christ, and present New Creation life — not escape, performance, or fear-based religion.

Project

  • FP Bible Standard
    The locked, non-negotiable format governing every chapter, book, and section in the FP Bible project.

Life · Thoughts · Audio

  • Thoughts

  • Audio
    Spoken reflections, readings, and music aligned with the Fulfilled / New Creation lens.
